Here be a nice margarita with honey and lime

Description: Honey lime margarita is a delicious cocktail made from your every day kitchen ingredients, while keeping its qualities as a margarita such as providing you withall five tastes

Ingredients:

(2 servings)
  • lime wedges for rubbing on the rim and for garnish
  • chili lime seasoning or margarita salt
  • ice
  • 1/2 cup tequila silver or blanco
  • 1/2 cup lime juice freshly squeezed
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons honey syrup or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rim two drinking glasses with a wedge of lime before dipping the wet rim into a small plate with chili lime seasoning / margarita salt. Fill each glass full with ice.
